🧳 journeybot-app - Plan your next trip with ease

Download Journeybot

Journeybot helps you organize your travels. It tracks your destinations and keeps your packing lists in one place. You can manage your entire vacation from your Apple devices.

📋 Features

  • Trip Planner: Map out your daily activities and travel dates.
  • Packing Lists: Create checklists for your bags to stay organized.
  • Device Sync: Keep your data consistent across iPhone, iPad, and Mac.
  • Smart Alerts: Receive notifications about upcoming flight times or local weather.

Ensure your device meets these conditions before you run the installer:
